About

SAARANG, the largest cultural festival of South India, started off by the students of Sastra, Thanjavur in the year 2019 now attracts students from various colleges. It has provided multiple talented artists to kick-start their careers while attracting them back year after year.The fest is known for bringing forth legends in the industry. SAARANG continues to create new friendships and long lasting memories. Join us next year from March 7th-8th for the celebration of art and culture like none other. Mark your calendars and save the dates as this year's edition will surpass all expectations.
